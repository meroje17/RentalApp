### Support

RentalApp is a iOS application with Swift language. Supporting : iOS 13.x and above, device orientation only portrait.

### Context

I created this application in order to help my parents with their rental. The goal here was to make them a digital showcase for their house rental. This app is on the App Store.

## Features

* Presenting house through multiple infos
  * Infos : details of house and price
  * Pictures : Lot of house pictures
  * Phone : phone number and possibilitie to call
  * Mail : the mail to reserve
  * Envelop : the location of the house
* Around button -> To present all activities around the house :
  * To visit / Restaurants / Tasting / Beach / Services / Markets
  * Infos of each of us, with button to open itinary in Google Maps
